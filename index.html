How Much Does a Boat Rental Cost Lake Travis?


Looking to rent a boat on Lake Travis? Whether you’re interested in fishing, sightseeing, or just getting out on the water, there are plenty of options available to suit your needs. But how much does it cost to rent a boat on Lake Travis? What is the average cost of a boat rental on Lake Travis?


The average cost of a boat rental on Lake Travis is $100 to $200 per hour. This price range depends on the size and type of boat you want to rent, as well as the length of time you want to rent it for. Some companies also offer discounts for renting multiple boats or for longer periods of time. If you are looking to rent a party boat with a captain on Lake Travis then expect to pay between $900 to $1500 for 4 hours. Be prepared to pay the captain a tip of 20% of the total booking price.


What are some factors that affect the cost of a boat rental?


There are several factors that affect the cost of a boat rental. The type of boat, the length of the rental, and the number of people renting all play a role in how much you’ll pay.

If you’re looking for a simple pontoon boat to enjoy a day on the lake with your family, you can expect to pay around $100 to $200 per hour. But if you want something more luxurious, like a yacht or speedboat, be prepared to spend a lot more.

The length of your rental also affects cost. Most boat rentals are charged by the hour, so if you want to rent for longer than just a few hours, you may be able to get a discount. And finally, the number of people renting also impacts price. If you have a large group, you’ll likely get a discount on your overall rate.


How can I save money on a boat rental?


There are a number of ways to save money on a boat rental at Lake Travis. One way is to search for discounts and deals online. Another way is to find a rental company that offers discounts for longer rentals or for multiple boats. Additionally, many companies offer Military or AAA discounts. Finally, it’s always helpful to ask the rental company if they have any current specials or promotions that could save you money on your rental.


What are some things to do on Lake Travis?


Assuming you’re referring to activities that can be done ON the lake, here are a few popular options:

tubing – Tubes can be rented out from most marinas for around $20/hour. This is a great option for groups of friends or families with small children.

fishing – The lake is home to many different types of fish, including bass, catfish, and sunfish. You can rent a boat with all the necessary gear, or fish from the shore.

sailing – Several companies offer sailboat rentals by the hour. This is a great activity for couples or groups of friends looking for a relaxing time on the lake.
